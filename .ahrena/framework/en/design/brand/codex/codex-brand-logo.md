# Codex: Logo, Standalone Marks, and Background Application

> **Prefix:** `codex-` | **Type:** Reference Manual | **Scope:** Guardia logo, symbol, and standalone mark

## Overview

The full visual signature (symbol + typography + colors) is the **brand mark**. The **logotype** is the written representation of the name with its own typographic treatment; when accompanied by the symbol, it forms the **brand signature**. This Codex consolidates the official variants, the meaning of the elements, and the application rules per background.

## Context

- **Domain:** Guardia's applied visual identity (logo + symbol + variations).
- **Target audience:** designers, marketing, sales, frontend/mobile, AI agents that produce branded pieces.
- **Update trigger:** when the *Logomarca e Logotipos* page in Notion is revised.

## Content

### Symbol and typography

#### The "G"

Central letter of the symbol. Constructed with rounded lines, firm contours, and a clean horizontal cut inside. The closed shape reinforces **protection and custody**; the inner cut keeps it open enough to convey **trust, accessibility, and transparency**.

#### The three blocks

Inspired by the Tetris L piece (*Orange Ricky*). At Guardia, we removed the fourth block to form an **arrow tip**: agentic accounting leading the accounting, financial, tax, and fiscal operation to a new level of autonomy, safety, and efficiency.

#### Typography: Lastica

Geometric sans-serif by Alberto Fontense. Chosen for clean geometry, modern/technical character, and legibility at different scales. Reserved exclusively for the construction of the logos and official brand applications.

### Logos (symbol + "Guardia" wordmark)

#### Primary logo

- **Composition:** violet square with orange "G" followed by "Guardia" in Lastica.
- **When to use:** institutional materials, commercial presentations, external communication, market touchpoints.
- **Backgrounds:** preferably light backgrounds; allowed on dark backgrounds outside the violet spectrum.
- **Do not use on:** backgrounds within the violet spectrum (symbol blends with background).

#### Secondary logo

- **Composition:** orange square with violet "G" followed by "Guardia" in orange Lastica.
- **When to use:** on backgrounds in the violet spectrum (deep violet, purple, lilac).
- **Do not use on:** orange, amber, coral, or warm-tone backgrounds (use the primary).

#### Monochrome black

- **Composition:** symbol + "Guardia" entirely in black.
- **When to use:** B&W prints, official documents without color support, contracts, fax, seals, technical engravings.
- **Backgrounds:** light (white, off-white, light gray).

#### Monochrome white

- **Composition:** symbol + "Guardia" entirely in white.
- **When to use:** dark or saturated backgrounds where the others lack contrast.
- **Backgrounds:** black, dark gray, deep violet, low-luminance photos/textures.

### Standalone marks (symbol only, no wordmark)

Reduced applications (favicons, app icons, social avatars, compact signatures) or contexts where the brand is already established.

#### Violet mark

- Violet "G" without backing square or wordmark.
- Light or orange backgrounds ensure contrast; reserved for those contexts.

#### Orange mark

- Orange "G" without backing square or wordmark.
- Backgrounds in the violet spectrum or dark backgrounds ensure contrast.

#### Monochrome black mark

- Black "G" without backing or wordmark. Light backgrounds.

#### Monochrome white mark

- White "G" without backing or wordmark. Dark or saturated backgrounds.

#### Violet mark (transparent background)

- Applications over warm tones (orange, amber, coral) or images with warm dominance.

#### Orange mark (transparent background)

- Applications over violet-spectrum tones or images with violet dominance.

### Quick selection by background

| Background | Recommended variant |
|------------|---------------------|
| White / off-white / light gray | Primary logo *or* monochrome black |
| Deep violet / purple / lilac | Secondary logo *or* orange transparent mark |
| Black / dark gray | Monochrome white *or* white mark |
| Orange / amber / coral | Primary logo *or* violet transparent mark |
| B&W (technical print) | Monochrome black |
| Favicon / avatar / compact signature | Standalone mark (variant per background) |

### General rules

- Use exclusively the official files.
- Do not recolor, distort, rotate, apply outlines, shadows, gradients, or effects.
- Do not replace Lastica with another font to "rebuild" the logo.
- Respect the documented minimum size (clear-space and minimum-size defined in the kit).
- In product, consume the `<Logo />` component exposed by `@guardia/design-system`.

# Lexis: Brand Voice — Direct, Strategic, Affirmative, Clear

> **Prefix:** `lex-` | **Type:** Unbreakable Law | **Scope:** All written and verbal communication on behalf of Guardia

## Law

> **All communication on behalf of Guardia (site, posts, emails, proposals, contracts, microcopy, support, internal communication, Isac messages) MUST follow the 4 voice pillars: Direct, Strategic, Affirmative, and Clear. Buzzwords without meaning (innovative, disruptive, transformative, revolutionary, synergy, alignment as an empty noun), promises to "revolutionize/transform" without concrete outcome, or describing Guardia as a "fintech" are FORBIDDEN. The positioning is "agentic accounting." When listing capabilities, the sequence accounting → financial → tax → fiscal MUST be respected. Statements MUST be backed by data, evidence, or verifiable references.**

## Coverage

- **Applies to:** site, landing pages, posts (LinkedIn, Instagram, X), commercial and transactional emails, proposals, contracts, decks, technical documentation, UI microcopy, Isac messages, support, internal communication, releases.
- **Bound agents:** marketing, sales, customer success, legal, product, support, designers, frontend, AI agents that generate public-facing or client-facing text (UX/copy warriors and cries).
- **Exceptions:** verbatim third-party quotes (preserved with quotation marks); mandatory legal terms in contracts; proper names and registered trademarks. Every exception in a public piece requires Brand review.

## Examples

### Correct

> "Guardia is an agentic accounting platform that automates accounting, financial, tax, and fiscal operations with AI agents auditing every move."

> "We reduced monthly close time by 70% for an accounting firm with 5,000 clients."

> "Guardia is direction, motion, and progress."

### Incorrect

> "Guardia is an innovative infrastructure revolutionizing the accounting market with disruptive AI solutions." *(empty buzzwords)*

> "We deliver transformative operational efficiency to our clients." *(vague promise, no data)*

> "Guardia is not a static fit; it is direction." *(negative construction before the affirmation)*

> "Guardia is the fintech that…" *(incorrect positioning)*

## Automated Validation

- **Tooling:** review by copy agent (warrior-hermes or similar Brand agent) detecting forbidden buzzwords and "not X, but Y" patterns; custom Markdown content lint; final Brand checklist before public publication.
- **Timing:** content PR review (site, blog, microcopy); Brand review for commercial pieces; review before paid campaigns.
- **Metric:** 0 occurrences of "innovative," "disruptive," "transformative," "revolutionary," "fintech" in official pieces (except quotes); 100% of impact claims backed by verifiable data; sequence accounting → financial → tax → fiscal preserved when applicable.
